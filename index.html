About ME

Hello, I'm Pranav P R, a dedicated color pencil portrait artist. My passion lies in bringing faces to life, exploring the nuanced expressions and intricate details that make each individual unique. Using professional-grade color pencils on premium Bristol board, I build layers upon layers of pigment to achieve hyper-realistic textures and vibrant, emotional depth. Each portrait is a journey of meticulous observation and careful rendering, aiming to capture not just a likeness, but the very essence and soul of the subject. I believe that a great portrait should tell a story, and I strive for every piece to resonate with beauty, character, and timeless artistry.
